Overview

Souls for Souls is a small group of highly motivated individuals thriving to contribute towards top priority needs of the world. We believe technology can help us solve most complex problems in order to make the world most beautiful for each and every soul.

Mission

We want to bridge the gap between two ends of society. Our primary mission is to utilize technology in order to enable society to contribute food to most vulnerable people. It also channelise demand and over food production in the world.

Goals

  • To provide software application that enables a person in need to connect with generous food donor
  • To eliminate food wastage all around the world by channelizing it
  • To facilitate donor to discover nearest hungry person
